Guacamole Lab

Description

We call this a lab instead of a recipe because you’re going to be experimenting! What are the flavors you like best in your guacamole? Add them! If you’re not sure, put a spoonful of the guacamole on a plate, then add just a pinch of your chosen spice or ingredient.

If you like it, consider seasoning the whole batch that way. Serve the guacamole with whole-grain chips or vegetables for dipping—or use it to top nachos, tacos, burritos, or quesadillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 perfectly ripe avocado, peeled, pitted, and cut into large chunks
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Put the avocado in the bowl and mash it with the fork until it has the texture you like best.
  2. Add the lime juice, salt, and any additional ingredients you like (see below) and mash again. Taste the guacamole. Does it need a bit more seasoning or a squeeze more lime juice? Add it and then taste again. Serve right away.
